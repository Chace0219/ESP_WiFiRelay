Hold IO port active during power-up and watch the blue light at the board blinking during power up. Keep holding the IO active for 4 seconds after the blue light stops blinking and release. The most simple way to activate IO is to connect 9V battery to the IO terminals (observe polarity). Use longer wires to avoid interference caused by hands touching the board. You can use also the power source at the board but keep in mind that grounds are separated for the board and IO terminals.
